Smart India Hackathon 2025

Posted by: Ministry of Education, India · Posted on: June 1, 2025

About This Opportunity

Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a nationwide initiative to provide students with a platform to solve pressing problems faced by our country. It encourages out-of-the-box thinking and innovation, bringing together some of the brightest young minds across every technical and non-technical field in India.

Participating teams of up to six members will work intensively over 36 hours to build a functional prototype that addresses a real-world problem statement provided by government ministries, departments, and industries. Mentors from IITs and leading companies will be on-site to guide teams throughout the hackathon.

Winners are awarded cash prizes, certificates of excellence, and direct visibility with industry recruiters who serve as judges. Past participants have gone on to secure internships, pre-placement offers, and even seed funding for their ideas.

Registration is open to undergraduate and postgraduate students from any recognized college or university in India. Teams must register through their institution's nodal centre before the deadline.
